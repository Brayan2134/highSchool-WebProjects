My Favorite Sites.

The sites below are a collection of sites that I think are really clean. While over time I may find other sites that are much better deisgned maintained, or have the best features, as it stands as of November 13, 2018, these are my personal list. If you scroll down to the bottom of the page or look to the right you can find the information in my best form, as a showcase to my true skill!

Bare Bowls.

Barebowls. This is a very clean website with the minimalist approach. There's nothing that's a standout feature of this site, but this website tries to have as little as possible, going to the extent of font size.

Squarespace.

We've all heard about it, yet very few people have actually visited the website. I don't need to explain how good SquareSpace is, because I don't think of words to describe it.

Melaine F.

This is just an amazing ASPX/JSX website. Not only was the website made with very little HTML, but evey little section of the website was taken care of. Besides the responsiveness, the speed of which everything was made out of is unreal, while still keeping a clear message via the e-commerce store.
